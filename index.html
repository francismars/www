About Me

Hi, I'm Francis, a software developer passionate about building practical tools with real-world impact. I developed Chain Duel, a Lightning-powered videogame, and PubPay, a simple way to split payments or receive donations via Lightning on Nostr. My work often explores the intersection of tech and permissionless money. I enjoy creating projects that are fun, useful, and aligned with open-source values. When I'm not at the computer, you'll likely find me lifting weights, hiking, or just spending time outdoors getting sun and fresh air.
